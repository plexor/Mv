What is the MAFIAVERSE

Mafiaverse is a roleplay game built on the Sandbox metaverse. The game is a mafia roleplay MMO inspired in the dynamics and lore of the mafia families of New York city in the 1930's. The game wouldn't consist of a linear script but an umbrella of different activities that generate value for the player. The point of the game is based around the role that you are partaking, a mobster will look to become the strongest mafia, while a cop must try to stop that from happening. For this there are different features that are based around an NFT badge system that enables you to do an specific set of quests depending of your role.

NFTs Pre-Sale

We will implement a whitelist system in which the players will have to meet certain requirements: A specified level on the project discord server, invites, social media shares, still not decided how it will work. Details on this later. On completion of these requirements, you will be awarded whitelist status which will make you eligible for different raffles. Additionally, you will be able to participate in the presale of real estate and other in-game objects or collectibles, which will be sold at a discount compared to the public sale. Participants of the presale will be eligible for airdrops and other giveaways as the mint progresses, the rewards and airdrops specifics are still to be determined.

Casino & Other Cash Flowing Activities Launch

We want to launch a reduced version of the game with a few mafia related quests so that players can get an initial taste of the roleplay experience and game mechanics. We aim to deliver an exclusive casino experience with different types of slot machines (money and NFT prizes), dice game, roulette, and a few others. Nevertheless, we have recently found out that The Sandbox Developers won’t approve purely randomness-based gambling games right now as they want to be in “full compliance” (we assume this has to do with the weird ways we have to employ right now to generate randomness due to the game maker logic limitations). This is scheduled to be delivered somewhere between late January and Early February, we will give further updates on this and on gambling games shortly.
